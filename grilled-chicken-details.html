Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

1. Marinate the chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.

2. Heat the grill and cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side.

3. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!
